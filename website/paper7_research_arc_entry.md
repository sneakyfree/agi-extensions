# Paper 7 — Research Arc Card

**Badge:** 7
**Title:** The Throughput Basin Origin
**Subtitle:** Different data, different basin

**Card text:**
The ~4 bits-per-token throughput floor isn't a property of the model — it's a property of the text. When we train SYN-8, an architecturally identical model, on synthetic 8-bit-entropy data, it tracks the new ceiling instead of collapsing to ~4 BPT. The basin moves with the data. The wall was a mirror.
